# Blueberry Muffins

Variation of Margaret Goode's buttermilk blueberry muffin recipe 

![Blueberry Muffins](../img/BlueberryMuffins.jpg)

## Ingredients

### Wet Mix 

- ¼ cup Shortening
- ⅔ cup Sugar, white
- 1 each Eggs
- ¾ cup Buttermilk

### Dry Mix 

- 2 cups Flour, AP
- 1 teaspoons Baking Powder
- ½ teaspoon Soda 
- ½ teaspoon Salt

### Blueberries 

- 1 cup Blueberries, frozen 

## Instructions

- In a medium stainless bowl, combine wet ingredients
- In a small bowl, combine the dry ingredients
- Incorporate the dry mix into the wet mix to combine. Stir gently.
- Fold in the blueberries
- Place a scoop of dough into muffin cups (14) and sprinkle with sugar
- Bake at 400℉ 18 minutes
