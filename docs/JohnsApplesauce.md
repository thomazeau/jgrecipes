# John's Applesauce

![Applesauce](img/applesauce.jpg)


## Ingredients

- 6 each Apples
- ¼ teaspoon Cinnamon
- ½ cup Sugar
- 1 cup Apple juice

## Instructions

- Rinse apples. Remove skins and core. Slice into segments and place in a pot.
- Place skins & cores in another pot and boil in ½ cup of water for 5 minutes.
- Pass through collandar into pot with apples. Discard skins & seeds.
- Add all ingredients.
- Cover and cook on element 15 minutes at 500W / 90 degree C.
- Let sit to cool after cooking.
- Store in refrigerator.

## Notes
- Great on toast with butter...!!!!  
- Cooking with the skins gives a nice pink tint.  
- Add pulp from crab apples when available or cranberry sauce to enhance colour.
