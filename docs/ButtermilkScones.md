# Buttermilk Scones

![Buttermilk Scones](img/ButtermilkScones.jpg)

This recipe contains an egg. Best used for sweeter biscuits, for dessert. 

### Ingredients

- **Dry mix:**
  - 2½ cups Flour
  - 2½ teaspoons Baking powder
  - ½ teaspoon Soda
  - ½ teaspoon Salt
  - ½ cup Butter, cubed, cold
  - ½ cup Raisins, Cranberries, Orange peel
- **Buttermilk mix:**
  - 1 egg
  - 2 Tbs Sugar
  - 1 cup Buttermilk (3/4 cup of milk with 1 tablespoon of vinegar)
  - 1 tablespoon Cream, for brushing

### Instructions

- Prepare buttermilk mix in Pyrex cup or small bowl.
- In a large stainless bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, soda and salt. Cut in butter and chop with a pastry cutter to a coarse meal. Add buttermilk mix just until combined. Add currants & chopped peel.
- Preheat oven to 400℉
- Transfer dough to a floured board. Roll to 3/4 inch thick. Fold the dough in thirds. Repeat 2-3 times. 
- Cut each round with 2 3/4" cutter and place slightly separated on a greased/floured baking sheet. Brush the tops with the cream, sprinkle with raw sugar and bake until lightly browned.
- 400℉ for 22 minutes.
- Serve warm, split in half with butter and marmalade.
